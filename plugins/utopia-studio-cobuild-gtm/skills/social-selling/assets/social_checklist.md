# Social Selling Checklist

## Daily Routine (15-30 mins)
- [ ] **Review Notifications**: Check who viewed your profile or engaged with your posts.
- [ ] **Engage with 5 Prospects**: Like/Comment on their recent activity.
- [ ] **Send 3 Connection Requests**: Personalized notes only.
- [ ] **Respond to DMs**: Keep the conversation moving.
- [ ] **Check Signals**: Look for job changes or news in target accounts.

## Weekly Routine
- [ ] **Post Content**: Share 1-2 insights or stories relevant to your audience.
- [ ] **Update Target List**: Add new prospects, remove disqualified ones.
- [ ] **Review Analytics**: Which posts/DMs worked best?
- [ ] **Engage in Groups**: Comment in 1-2 relevant LinkedIn groups or communities.

## Metrics to Track
- **Connection Acceptance Rate**: Target > 30%
- **Reply Rate on DMs**: Target > 15%
- **Meetings Booked via Social**: Target > 2 per week
