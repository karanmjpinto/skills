# Discovery Call Question Bank

## Executive / Economic Buyer
**Focus:** Strategic alignment, ROI, risk mitigation.

- "How does this initiative align with the company's top 3 goals for this year?"
- "What happens if you do nothing about this problem for another 6 months?"
- "Who else cares about solving this problem, and how are they measuring success?"
- "How will you evaluate whether this investment was a success 12 months from now?"
- "Is there a budget allocated for this, or will we need to build a business case together?"

## Champion / Project Lead
**Focus:** Workflow, implementation, personal win.

- "Walk me through your current process for [X]. Where is the biggest bottleneck?"
- "What have you tried in the past to solve this? Why didn't it work?"
- "If you could wave a magic wand and fix one thing about your current workflow, what would it be?"
- "Who will be the primary users of the solution, and what will their main concerns be?"
- "What is your timeline for getting a solution in place?"

## Technical Buyer / IT
**Focus:** Security, integration, compliance, maintenance.

- "What are your requirements for security and compliance (e.g., SOC2, GDPR)?"
- "What other tools does this need to integrate with?"
- "How do you typically handle deployment and user provisioning?"
- "Do you have any specific data residency or privacy requirements?"
- "What is your process for vetting new vendors?"

## Objection Handling
- **"We're too busy right now."** -> "I understand. Is it that this isn't a priority, or just bad timing? If we could save you [X] hours per week, would that be worth a 15-minute conversation?"
- **"We already have a solution."** -> "Great. Most of our customers did too. What are the gaps in your current solution that you wish you could fill?"
- **"Send me some information."** -> "I'd be happy to. To make sure I send what's relevant, could you tell me which aspect of [problem] is most pressing for you right now?"
