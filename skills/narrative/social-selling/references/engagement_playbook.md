# Social Engagement Playbook

## Core Principles
1.  **Insight First**: Don't just "like". Add value with a comment.
2.  **Timely Engagement**: Engage when the topic is hot.
3.  **Social Proof**: Leverage mutual connections.
4.  **Call-to-Value**: Offer insights, not just "chats".

## Engagement Ladder
1.  **Monitor**: Track target accounts and key stakeholders.
2.  **Micro-Engage**: Like and react to posts. (Low effort, low visibility).
3.  **Value Drops**: Comment with unique insights or data. (High effort, high visibility).
4.  **DM**: Direct message with a specific, relevant offer or question.
5.  **Follow-Through**: Move to email/call if positive response.

## Signal Tracking
| Signal | Action |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Hiring** | "Saw you're growing the [Team] team. How are you thinking about [Challenge]?" |
| **Promotion** | "Congrats on the new role! I imagine [Goal] is top of mind now?" |
| **Funding** | "Congrats on the Series B! exciting times ahead for [Company]." |
| **Event Attendance** | "Saw you're going to [Event]. I'll be there too, would love to say hi." |
| **Content Engagement** | "Thanks for liking my post on [Topic]. What resonated with you?" |

## DM Scripts
### The "Insight" DM
> "Hey [Name], loved your point about [Topic] in yesterday's thread.
>
> We just published a short breakdown on how [Peer Company] tackled [Challenge]. Happy to share it if you're curious.
>
> Worth a quick chat?"

### The "Mutual Connection" DM
> "Hey [Name], I see we both know [Mutual Connection].
>
> I've been following [Company]'s work on [Project] and thought you might find this [Resource] interesting given your focus on [Topic].
>
> Open to connecting?"
